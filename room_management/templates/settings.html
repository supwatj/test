Help
Why are these settings important?

These settings determine how room vacancies are calculated for your reports.

Example Scenario:

If a guest checks out on February 3rd, and your early day is 5, that room counts as vacant for February. If they checkout on February 28th, it counts as not vacant.

Changes to these settings will immediately affect the vacancy report calculations.
